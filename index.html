About Aurelia

Crafting Homes, Not Houses

Welcome to Aurelia, your new abode for finding curated, elegant, and timeless home decor. Born from a passion for artistry and the quiet comfort of a well-designed space, Aurelia (from Latin 'aureus' - golden) represents the golden touch we aim to bring to your home.

We partner with independent artisans and the finest workshops to source items that tell a story. From hand-thrown pottery to sustainably sourced linen, every piece in our collection is chosen for its quality, beauty, and ability to transform a room.

Our mission is simple: to bring you a collection that feels both modern and timeless, allowing you to build a home that is uniquely yours.
